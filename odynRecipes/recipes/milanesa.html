Milanesa recipe

  • 4 thin beef steaks
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying or olive oil for baking

Preparation steps

  1. Season the steaks with salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Dip each steak in the beaten eggs
  3. Coat with breadcrumbs, pressing firmly so they stick
  4. Fried: Heat plenty of oil in a pan and fry each milanesa for 3-4 minutes per side until golden and crispy
  5. Baked: Place on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil and bake at 200°C for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through
  6. Serve hot and enjoy! 🍽️
