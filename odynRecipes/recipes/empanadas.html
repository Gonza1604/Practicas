Empanada recipe

Empanadas are traditional Latin American pastries filled with seasoned meat, vegetables or cheese, wrapped in a crispy golden dough and baked or fried to perfection. A classic comfort food enjoyed across Argentina and the rest of South America.

ingredients for dougth

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, sliced
  • Green olives
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation steps

  1. Place a generous spoonful of cold filling in the center of the dough disc
  2. Moisten the edges with a little water, fold the dough and press to seal. Make the traditional fold or use a fork to crimp the edges
  3. Brush with beaten egg and bake at high temperature (200°C - 220°C) for 15 to 20 minutes until golden, or fry in plenty of hot oil until the dough bubbles and browns
  4. Enjoy your homemade empanadas while still warm!
